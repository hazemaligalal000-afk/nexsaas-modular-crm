# NexSaaS CRM — Privacy Policy (PRIVACY_POLICY.md)
# (Requirement 8: Privacy Policy)

## 📊 Data Collection (Requirement 8.2-8.3)
We collect:
1. **User Identity Profile**: Name, email, and IP address for account security.
2. **Company Meta**: Industry, brand colors, and company name.
3. **Usage Telemetry**: We use anonymous metrics to improve AI Engine performance.

## 🔒 Storage & Protection (Requirement 8.4-8.5)
All data is stored in **Symmetric AES-256-GCM** encrypted format. (Requirement 12.5)
Retention Period: **7 years** for financial/tax records (ZATCA compliance) and **90 days** for non-essential audit logs. (Requirement 13.9)

## 🇪🇺 GDPR Compliance (Requirement 8.10)
For EU users, we provide:
1. **Right to Access**: Download your entire tenant DB (JSON). (Requirement 8.6)
2. **Right to Deletion**: Complete 'Wipe Out' service for any deleted tenant. (Requirement 8.7)
3. **DPA**: We enter into a Data Processing Agreement with all Enterprise clients. (Requirement 8.10)

## 🍪 Cookies (Requirement 8.9)
We use only **Essential Session Cookies** to maintain secure login status. No third-party tracking or advertising scripts are used. (Requirement 8.9)

## 🔄 Third-Party Processors (Requirement 8.8)
1. **Stripe**: For card processing.
2. **OpenAI / Anthropic**: For AI drafting and intent analysis.
3. **Soketi**: Hosted WebSocket infrastructure. (Requirement 8.8)
